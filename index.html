What Makes Amarillo CoolSculpting Different?

When it comes to non-invasive body sculpting, CoolSculpting stands out for its simplicity and science-backed method. The technology, based on cryolipolysis, works by safely applying controlled cooling to fat cells beneath the skin. These cells freeze and are gradually removed by the body’s natural processes over time.

We don’t take a one-size-fits-all approach. At our Amarillo location, each client undergoes a detailed consultation. Who Is a Good Fit for CoolSculpting?

CoolSculpting isn’t for weight loss. It’s a solution for those who are already near their ideal weight but are struggling with persistent fat in specific areas like the:

  • Abdomen
  • Flanks (“love handles”)
  • Inner or outer thighs
  • Under the chin
  • Upper arms
  • Back and bra line
  • Underneath the buttocks

If you’re maintaining a healthy lifestyle but still dealing with these common concerns, you might be a candidate. We always recommend a one-on-one consultation so we can determine if it’s a safe and effective option for you.

  2. Treatment

What Does Science Say?

CoolSculpting is backed by over a decade of research and clinical testing. It’s FDA-cleared, non-invasive, and has a proven track record of success. According to clinical studies, fat cells that are frozen during treatment are reduced by an average of 20-25% in the treated area.

We’ve seen it work firsthand in our Amarillo location time and time again. While results vary, most of our clients begin to notice changes within three weeks, with full results typically seen in two to three months.

Because the body continues to flush out fat cells over time, results look natural and develop gradually. This often means friends and family may notice you’re looking refreshed—without knowing exactly what’s changed.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Is the treatment painful?

Most clients report minimal discomfort. You may feel intense cold and tugging at the start, but it usually subsides within a few minutes.

Q: How many sessions will I need?

This varies from person to person. Some may be satisfied with just one session, while others may need a series of treatments. During your consultation, we’ll help outline the best plan for your needs.

Q: Are the results permanent?

The fat cells treated are permanently removed. However, it’s still important to maintain a healthy lifestyle, as remaining fat cells can still expand if weight is gained.

Q: Is there any downtime?

No. You can return to your regular activities immediately after your appointment.
